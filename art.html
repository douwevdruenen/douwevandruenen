Digital Art

For as long as I can remember, I've loved to draw. Recently, I've started drawing digitally. My art mostly revolves around character design, with one of my most unique characters being Tsumi, seen in the artwork above. I mostly work with Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Fresco, and some backgrounds are AI generated. Below are artworks of a few of my original characters.

Serena

This character is part of a world-building project I'm working on. I'm not sure if I'll ever share it with the world, but I'm still working on it for fun. This is one of my favourite characters.

Navi

This is the main character of my project, he is a soldier who figures out he is the god of lightning. This drawing is actually a second attempt. I took the Illustrator file of the original drawing and redid some of the effects and details from what I've learned since I originally drew it.

Tempestia

The goddess of time. The twelve chains represent the hours on a clock. With this drawing I used AI to generate the background. I then matched the lighting to blend everything together.

Akira

This character lives in a dystopian future. I just wanted to experiment with different settings, but I liked this character so much, that I worked her into my project.

Jun

Jun is not part of any project. I drew her for the first time a long time ago in the back of my math notebook, and I wanted to see how I'd draw her now. I also liked drawing something more modern and artistic. I also experimented with new details like blush and a more abstract background.

White Queen

This character is based on the chess piece. I first made this character for my animation elective. I liked her design so much, I decided to draw her in a full artwork. I drew her in Adobe Illustrator and made the background and some of the effects in Adobe Fresco.
