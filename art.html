Digital Art

For as long as I can remember I've loved to draw. A few years ago I started drawing digitally as well. My art mostly revolves around character design, as I love world-building and creating characters to inhabit those worlds. Most of my work is of characters from a world I've been working on for years. I make my work using Adobe Fresco. Below are some of my artworks of original characters.

Serena

This character is part of a world-building project I'm working on. I'm not sure if I'll ever share it with the world, but I'm still working on it for fun. This is one of my favourite characters.

Jun and Kaede

Jun started out as a stand-alone character, but I developed a new project around her, including a new character named Kaede. They hunt yokai in a future version of Tokyo, which I found to be an interesting setting for a story. I really enjoyed designing their clothing, because it's a very different style than I'm used to.

Navi

This is the main character of one of my projects, he is an elven soldier who finds out he is the god of lightning. I used this artwork to rework the armour designs of the nation he's from. I also liked making this artwork more cinematic than I usually do.

Axelia Zephyr

Axelia Zephyr is the daughter of Senator Hesperis Zephyr, a very influential man. She is human, unlike many of my other characters. She is a driven and skilled warrior who proudly fights for her homeland. She cares little about her noble status and works hard to achieve her goals. This character was first created so the overarching story could be viewed from the perspective of what the other characters regard as the enemy.

Lyra

Lyra is another human character who is a dear friend of Axelia. She comes from humble beginnings, but managed to make a name for herself as a skilled healer. She can be hotheaded and stubborn, but she is always there for those who need her. She has a rich cultural background within the world I created. Her clothing, armour, piercings, face painting and even her weoponry originate from these different cultures she is a part of.

Danji

Danji is the character I play as in a Dungeons & Dragons campaign. He is a changeling, meaning that he can shapeshift. I really like this character, as he has a very rich backstory.

Caelum

The god of space and the sky. He was heavily wounded in the war that got him banished from the pantheon. The single wing on his right and the three arms on his left create an interesting silhouette.

Desideria

Desideria is the demigoddess of lust and desire. On top of that, she is the Queen of a powerful nation. I wanted to make her look regal, so I gave her a lot of golden jewelry and a crown based on ancient Egyptian regents. The usage of pink and red tones symbolise her divine powers.
