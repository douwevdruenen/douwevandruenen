Digital Art

For as long as I can remember I've loved to draw. A few years ago I started drawing digitally as well. My art mostly revolves around character design, as I love world-building and creating characters to inhabit those worlds. Most of my characters are from a world I've been working on for many years. Below are some of my artworks of original characters.

Serena

Serena is one of my favourite characters. She's the daughter of a crime boss, but has left that life behind her long ago. After a brief period of crimefighting as a vigilante, she now keeps her city safe as part of the city guard.

Jun and Kaede

Jun started out as a stand-alone character, but I developed a new project around her, including a new character named Kaede. They hunt yokai in a alternate reality of Tokyo in the year 2052. I enjoyed designing their clothing, as it's a very different style from what I'm used to.

Navi

Navi is a dedicated elven soldier with a complicated past. I used this artwork to re-establish the armour design of the nation he's from.

Axelia Zephyr

Axelia Zephyr is the daughter of Senator Hesperis Zephyr, a very influential man. She is human, unlike many of my other characters. She is a driven and skilled warrior who proudly fights for her homeland. She cares little about her noble status and works hard to achieve her goals. This character was first created so the overarching story could be viewed from an opposing perspective.

Lyra

Lyra is another human character who is a dear friend of Axelia. She comes from humble beginnings, but managed to make a name for herself as a skilled warrior. She can be hotheaded and stubborn, but she is always there for those who need her. She has a rich cultural background within the world I created. Her clothing, armour, piercings, face paintings and even her weoponry originate from the different cultures she's part of.

Danji

Danji is the character I played as in a Dungeons & Dragons campaign. He is a changeling, meaning that he can shapeshift. His character arc revolves around finding his own identity and trusting those around him. With this artwork I experimented with animated transitions between his different forms.

Luna

Goddess of the Moon and Night. Luna was corrupted by the Goddess of the Void, which is reflected in this design. While wearing emblems resembling the moon, her skin reflects the night sky instead. Older generations of Gods in my world have six arms, inspired by Hindu deities. In this artwork I wanted to show that these arms can physically interact with objects and even with each other, instead of just being symbolical.

Caelum

The God of Space and the Sky. He was heavily wounded in the war that got him banished from the pantheon. The single wing on his right and the three arms on his left create an interesting silhouette.

Desideria

Desideria is the Demigoddess of Lust and Desire. On top of that, she is the queen of a powerful nation. To make her look regal I gave her a lot of golden jewelry and a crown based on ancient Egyptian regents. The usage of pink and red tones symbolise her divine powers.
