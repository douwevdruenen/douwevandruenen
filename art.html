Digital Art

For as long as I can remember I've loved to draw. A few years ago I started drawing digitally as well. My art mostly revolves around character design, as I love world-building and creating characters to inhabit those worlds. I mostly work with Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Fresco, and some backgrounds are AI generated. Below are some of my best artworks so far.

Serena

This character is part of a world-building project I'm working on. I'm not sure if I'll ever share it with the world, but I'm still working on it for fun. This is one of my favourite characters.

Jun and Kaede

Jun started out as a stand-alone character, but I developed a new project around her, including a new character named Kaede. They hunt yokai in a future version of Tokyo, which I found to be an interesting setting for a story. This drawing is made in a style I adapted most recently.

Navi

This is the main character of one of my projects, he is an elven soldier who finds out he is the god of lightning. This drawing is actually a second attempt. I took the Illustrator file of the original drawing and redid some of the effects and details from what I'd learned since I originally drew it.

Tempestia

The goddess of time. The twelve chains represent the hours on a clock. I like to challenge myselfd, so I decided to put the primary light scource beneath the character. With this drawing I used AI to generate the background. I then matched the lighting to blend the character and the background together.

Jinx

Jinx is a character from the Netflix series Arcane. I really liked her design, so I decided to draw her. It was interesting to see how the character looked in my own artstyle, and eventhough this style is partly inspired by Arcane's artstyle, the artwork still looks unique.

King Vesana

This character is a female pirate King. With this drawing I combined a few different styles to create a new look. I liked drawing this character, as she seems pretty unhinged.

White Queen

This character is based on the chess piece. I first made this character for my animation elective. I liked her design so much, I decided to draw her in a full artwork. I drew her in Adobe Illustrator and made the background and some of the effects in Adobe Fresco.
