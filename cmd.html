Multimedia Design

I am, above all, a designer. I study Communication & Multimedia Design (CMD) at Zuyd Hogeschool Maastricht, where I've picked up skills like visual design, UX-design, animation and many more. I also study coding and programming, a skill I use often. This website is an example of that. Besides my studies, I've also done projects for other organisations. Below are a few projects I've worked on as a multimedia designer.

Code4Kids

At the end of my first year at CMD, we did a project where we had to supply a workshop for grade school students to teach them basic coding. That's where Codingcat was born. I was mostly responsible for the visual design on the project. I designed the logo, the website, a boardgame, and much more. We also gave the workshop to a full class of 12-year-olds, which was a lot of fun.

Multimedia Story

In my third year at CMD, I had an internship at the Centraal Bureau voor de Statistiek (CBS). Here I worked on a project to make a multimedia story about the European Green Deal. I was primarily responsible for the illustrations and animations of the story. You can click this link to view the story yourself.

During my internship, I also designed posters for a presentation at the Dutch Ministry of Foreign Affairs, where they even asked to keep the posters. After my internship, I was asked to make a new batch of posters for a convention, a project I gladly took on.

Generative Art

In my first year at CMD, I did a project where I programmed generative art, that randomly makes a moving art piece within a strict ruleset. It was very fun to work on, and I even got to use my visuals to accompany a live jazz band. here.

Knip Musical

The high school I used to go to has its own musical group that performs a musical every year in the biggest theatre in Helmond. I personally know the producer, who approached me in 2022 for the design of the poster for their musical, Ik hou van jou. The following year I not only designed the poster for their new musical, Pinokkio, but I also designed their new logo.

In 2024 I continued my work for Knip Musical with the musical "Tranen van Smaragd", where I made designs for the poster, flyers, banners for in the theatre and a digital billboard next to a busy road in Helmond. The design I made were also featured on projections during the musical itself.
