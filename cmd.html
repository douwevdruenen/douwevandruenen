Multimedia Design

I am a designer at heart. I study Communication & Multimedia Design (CMD) at Zuyd University of Applied Sciences in Maastricht, where I've picked up many skills like graphic design, UX-design, animation, coding, motion design and more. Besides my studies, I've also done projects for other organisations. Below are a few projects I've worked on as a multimedia designer.

Royal in Motion

In my fourth year of CMD, I did a project for Royal Theater, an artistic cinema in Heerlen. They are undergoing a large renovation and wanted to reimagine their branding. Together with a classmate, I made the concept 'Royal in Motion'. The concept revolves around the motion of a film camera, the motion of innovation and the motion of the visitors coming together in the new Royal. I was mainly responsible for the motion design. We presented our project in the cinema on the big screen and the client was very positive about the result.

Codingcat

At the end of my first year at CMD, we did a project called 'Code4Kids' where we had to develop a workshop for grade school students to teach them basic coding. That's where Codingcat was born. I was mostly responsible for the visual design on the project. I designed the logo, the website, a boardgame, and much more. We also gave the workshop to a full class of 12-year-olds, which was a lot of fun.

Multimedia Story

In my third year at CMD, I had an internship at Centraal Bureau voor de Statistiek (CBS). Here I worked on a project to make a multimedia story about the European Green Deal. I was primarily responsible for the illustrations and animations of the story. You can click this link to view the story yourself.

During my internship, I also designed posters for a presentation at the Dutch Ministry of Foreign Affairs, where they even asked to keep the posters. After my internship, I was asked to make a new batch of posters for a convention, a project I gladly took on.

Generative Art

In my first year at CMD, I did a project where I programmed generative art, that randomly makes a moving art piece within a strict ruleset. It was very fun to work on, and I even got to use my visuals to accompany a live jazz band.

Knip Musical

The high school I used to go to has its own musical group that performs a musical every year in the biggest theatre in Helmond. I personally know the producer, who approached me in 2022 for the design of the poster for their musical, Ik hou van jou. The following year I not only designed the poster for their new musical, Pinokkio, but I also designed their new logo.

In 2024 I continued my work for Knip Musical with the musical "Tranen van Smaragd", where I made designs for the poster, flyers, banners for in the theatre and a digital billboard next to a busy road in Helmond. The design I made were also featured on projections during the musical itself.
