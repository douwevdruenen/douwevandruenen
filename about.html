About me

So, who am I?

I'm Douwe van Druenen, a Multimedia Designer in the broadest sense. Born and raised in Helmond, The Netherlands, I am currently pursuing my studies in Communication & Multimedia Design (CMD) at Zuyd University of Applied Sciences in Maastricht. My passion lies in creating effective design and captivating art that tell compelling stories. Through my work, I aim to blend creativity with functionality, delivering engaging experiences across various platforms. Whether it's graphic design, UX design, illustration, animation or motion design, I strive to push the boundaries of visual communication and bring ideas to life.
