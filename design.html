Graphic Design

I really enjoy visual design, so I spend some of my spare time designing a bunch of things. I make visuals of my own logo and brand and make album covers for my playlists. Nothing too serious, but I thought it'd be fun to show it here anyway.

I've also done work for a few clients as a freelancer.

Knip Musical

The high school I used to go to has its own musical group that performs a musical every year in the biggest theatre in Helmond. I personally know the producer, and he approached me for the design of the poster for their musical, Ik hou van jou. The following year I also designed the poster for their new musical, Pinokkio. On top of that I designed the new logo for Knip Musical.
