Animation

This page is dedicated to my animation work. I've always loved to watch animated movies and shows, but recently I've started to make animations myself. I've followed a few electives during my studies where I learned to make 2D and 3D animations. I also spent half a year making an animated short film. Below is some of my work in animation.

Thirst

In my second year of CMD, I followed an elective where I learned the fundamentals of 2D animation. I experimented with a lot of different animation styles and I eventually made a short animation, Thirst, about a girl living by a waterfall who is being watched by a mysterious creature. You can watch it here.

Had Je Maar

In my third year of CMD, I followed an external elective of Visual Communication called 'Storytelling Fiction', where I spent half a year making an animated short film. I had decided early on I wanted to adapt my story as a short animation. I wrote a scenario, which took up the first half of the elective.

After I was satisfied with the story and had decided on a visual style, I could finally start animating. I really enjoyed the process, and the end product, made in collaboration with my classmate Myko Thomas, turned out great. You can click the link to view the preview for Had Je Maar.
